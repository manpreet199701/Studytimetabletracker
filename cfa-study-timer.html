The golden rule

Notion notes are your primary material — read them fully, every time. Schweser comes second and goes fast because the content is already familiar.

Flashcards for older chapters belong in a separate 20-minute daily slot. Never mix them into your chapter session.

If you score >80% on the LOS quiz on the first attempt, skip Block 4 entirely and bank 5 minutes.
